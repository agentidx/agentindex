Yield Risk Score Methodology
Five risk components composited into a 0–100 score.
40pts
APY Suspiciousness
Unsustainable APY levels. >500% = near-certain temporary emission event.
20pts
Reward Dependency
Share of yield from reward tokens vs. organic base fees. >90% reward = protocol subsidizing yield.
15pts
TVL Size
Small TVL pools have higher rug risk and exit illiquidity. <$100K = max risk.
15pts
Protocol Risk
Underlying protocol's ZARQ Trust Score + crash probability. CRITICAL + high APY = trap.
10pts
Impermanent Loss
IL-exposed pools with low APY are a poor risk/reward proposition. Low APY doesn't justify LP volatility.
API Reference
All yield risk data available via machine-readable API.
# Yield Risk Score for a specific pool
GET https://zarq.ai/v1/yield/risk/{protocol}/{pool}

# All yield traps globally
GET https://zarq.ai/v1/yield/traps?min_apy=100&chain=Ethereum

# All pools for a protocol with risk scores
GET https://zarq.ai/v1/yield/protocol/aave-v3?sort_by=risk
