// API Reference

Machine-ready endpoints

All cascade risk data available via REST API. Free during Early Access — rate limited to 1,000 calls/day.

GET
/v1/crypto/cascade/simulate
Simulate a collapse cascade from any trigger token. Returns full propagation chain with impact scores and USD exposure.
GET
/v1/crypto/cascade/hotspots
Top systemic risk nodes ranked by hotspot score — structural weakness × crash probability × graph degree.
GET
/v1/crypto/cascade/graph
Full dependency graph export in D3.js-compatible format with all nodes, edges, and risk metadata.
POST
/v1/crypto/portfolio/analyze
Submit holdings, receive weighted risk score, collapse exposure %, cascade simulation, and prioritized recommendations.
POST
/v1/crypto/portfolio/crash-shield
Register a webhook URL to receive real-time POST alerts when your holdings enter collapse conditions.
GET
/v1/crypto/cascade/stats
Graph engine statistics — total nodes, edge count by type, load time, and available simulation scenarios.
