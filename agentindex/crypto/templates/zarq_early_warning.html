How Early Warnings Work
01
Distance-to-Default
Adapted from Merton's structural credit model. Measures how many standard deviations a token is from critical failure. Below 2.0 = danger zone. Below 1.0 = imminent risk.
02
Weakness Accumulation
Four structural signals tracked: trust score decay, volatility spike, DtD compression, and momentum collapse. Each confirmed weakness adds to the count. 3+ triggers CRITICAL.
03
Crash Probability
90-day forward probability combining DtD trajectory, TVL divergence, contagion exposure, and historical default patterns. Our model achieves 98% precision on historical defaults.
04
Track Record
Every warning is logged with a SHA-256 hash before market open. We track what happens after each warning is issued. No hindsight. No adjustments. Full transparency.
